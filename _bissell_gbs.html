Bissell — Below the Surface Facebook Contest
There's vacuum clean, and there's Bissell deep cleaning clean. They own the deep cleaning category, but the category of deep cleaning as a whole still had tons of room to grow. Bissell tasked us with creating a Facebook initiative to grow their fanbase, but more importantly, educate consumers on the need to deep clean their carpets. We did this by literally taking consumers deep inside their carpet and showing them the dirt they can`t see. We also rewarded consumers with weekly deep cleaners, and a grand prize scuba diving adventure in the Bahamas.
