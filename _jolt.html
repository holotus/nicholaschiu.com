Jolt — Up All Night
With everyone talking about how their energy product will wake you up, Jolt Caffeine Energy Gum needed to stand out from the crowd. So we positioned Jolt Gum as the gum that won't just wake you up, but will keep you up. This was done through a series of print ads and night long partnership with Nuit Blanche.
Nuit Blanche is a free one night contemporary art event that ends when the sun comes up. People are encourage to stay out late and explore the art exhibits across the city. To help people stay out late and see more of Nuit Blanche, Jolt Energy Gum create the Nuit Blanche all nighter survival kit. Inserted into the officaial programme for the night, the kit contains 2 pieces of gum, as well as a contest that can only be entered with enough Nuit Blanche exploration.
