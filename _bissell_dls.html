Bissell — Dirty Little Secrets Contest
Bissell wanted to engage consumers in a conversation about deep cleaning carpets. We did this by simply asking consumers a simple question. What dirty little secret do you have hiding in your carpet? Submitted secrets were seeded in a virtual carpet and viewable anonymously by anyone. Consumers were able to win deep cleaning prize packs, as well as a dirty little trip to Las Vegas.
